Welcome to CHRIST (Deemed to be University), Kengeri Campus

The event will be held on the sprawling campus of CHRIST (DEEMED TO BE UNIVERSITY). It nears the epitome of the serene environment that is required for a well-balanced educational and academic life. Spread across an area of 78.5 acres, it is a lush green campus with lots of amenities. The Mysore Horticulture Society, Lalbagh, Bangalore has awarded the Special Outstanding Prize of 'Best Ornamental Gardens' to Kengeri Campus for five years consecutively starting from 2014.
The transportation from Airport or Railway Station or any Bus stands to the place of accommodation has to be arranged and paid by all participants. A shuttle service will be provided by the organizer from the nearest Metro station to the campus.
